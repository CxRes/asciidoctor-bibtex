== Sample file 2: multiple authors ==

References can use different styles such as: (Lane and Smith 2000) or
Lane and Smith (2000).

Page numbers can be added: (Lane and Smith 2000, 89) or Lane and Smith (2000, 89-93).

A bit of pretext can be included too: (See Lane and Smith 2000, 89)

=== multiple refs ===

One citation can include several references, such as (Lane 2000; Lane and Smith 2000).

These can include pages and be in-text: Lane (2000, 78); Lane and Smith (2000, 89-93).

And include pretext: (See Lane 2000; Lane and Smith 2000, 89-93)

Note: citations cannot be split over lines; each citation must be on one line.
